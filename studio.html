The Studio

Design, practiced patiently.

Campos Goldberg is a full-service interior design studio on West 39th Street, founded on a simple conviction: rooms should be composed, not decorated.

Who We Are

Campos Goldberg Interiors is a collaborative design firm founded by two friends, ready to bring years of combined experience to craft timeless, tailored spaces.

Isadora Campos and Ashira Goldberg met at Pratt Institute, where their friendship began through long studio days, shared projects, and many conversations about design.

We’re so excited to start this journey — and to share the spaces, details, and ideas that shape our studio.

Philosophy

We believe luxury is not abundance — it is precision. The right object, in the right room, placed once and never questioned again.

Approach

One considered point of view per room — composed, not decorated, and made to endure.

Focus

Full-service interior design for residential and boutique commercial spaces, in New York and beyond.

The Process

Four Movements
i.

Listen

Every commission opens with conversation — about routine, ritual, and the objects already loved. We design nothing until we understand how mornings happen in your home.

ii.

Compose

Architecture, palette, and proportion are resolved as one drawing, not three. You review a complete point of view — never a mood board of maybes.

iii.

Craft

We commission the workrooms, ateliers, and mills directly — upholstery in Long Island City, plaster from Brooklyn, textiles from small European houses we have worked with for years.

iv.

Reveal

Installation happens in days, not months — art hung, beds made, flowers placed. You leave with keys and return to a finished life.
